Piracy Policy for @GamesEventRoBot

1. Scope of Policy

This Piracy Policy applies to all users of the @GamesEventRoBot and participants in any tournaments organized via the admin. By interacting with the bot or participating in a tournament, users agree to adhere to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Prohibition of Pirated Content

  • Unauthorized Copies: Users are strictly prohibited from using pirated or unauthorized copies of any game for participation in tournaments.
  • Game Ownership: All participants must own a legal and licensed version of the game they wish to play in tournaments.
  • Verification: The bot or tournament organizers may request proof of ownership (e.g., purchase receipts, account screenshots) at any time.

3. Consequences of Violating Piracy Rules

  • Disqualification: Any user found using pirated content will be immediately disqualified from the tournament.
  • Permanent Ban: Repeat offenders may face permanent bans from the bot and all future tournaments.
  • Reporting: Severe violations may be reported to the appropriate authorities or the game's developers.

4. Anti-Cheating Measures

  • Monitoring: The bot or tournament organizers may use tools to monitor for unauthorized software or game versions.
  • Fair Play: All participants must ensure their gameplay is fair and in compliance with the game developer's terms of service.

5. Liability

  • @GamesEventRoBot and its organizers are not responsible for any disputes arising from users who fail to comply with this policy.
  • Participants are solely responsible for ensuring their compliance with the game’s licensing requirements.

6. Reporting Violations

If you suspect a participant is using pirated content:

  • Use the /report command on the bot to notify the organizers.
  • Provide relevant evidence or screenshots to support your report.

7. Amendments to the Policy

@GamesEventRoBot reserves the right to update this Piracy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of significant changes through announcements in the bot.

This policy aims to ensure fair gameplay and protect the intellectual property rights of game developers.
