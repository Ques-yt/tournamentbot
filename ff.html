Free Fire Custom Room Tournament Rules

General Rules

  • Mode: Matches will be played in Battle Royale or Clash Squad mode, based on the format.
  • Custom Room: Room details will be shared 10 minutes before the match.
  • Map Selection: Maps (e.g., Bermuda, Kalahari, Purgatory) will be decided by the organizer.
  • Team Composition: Squads must have 4 players, with an optional substitute.
  • Winning Criteria: Winning The Match.

Gameplay Rules

  • Fair Play: Hacks, cheats, or third-party software are prohibited.
  • Teaming: Teaming with other teams is not allowed.
  • Bug Exploits: Exploiting bugs or glitches is forbidden.
  • Communication: In-game communication and by local call is allowed.

Weapon & Item Restrictions

  • Certain weapons/items (e.g., M82B, grenade launchers) may be restricted as per the organizer.

Match Timing

  • Players must join the custom room 10 minutes before the start time.
  • Delays due to technical issues will be handled by the organizer.

Disqualification

  • Teams may be disqualified for rule violations, unauthorized players, or unsportsmanlike conduct.

Reporting Results

  • Submit screenshots or screen recording of match results to the organizer. Decisions by the organizer are final.

Prizes

  • Details will be announced by the organizer.

Note: Rules are subject to change and will be communicated before the tournament begins.
